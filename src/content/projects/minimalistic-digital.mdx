## Overview
Minimalistic Digital is a clockface designed exclusively for Fitbit Versa 3 and Fitbit Sense, embodying simplicity and elegance. With its minimalist aesthetic, this clockface offers users a clean and uncluttered display, enhancing readability and user experience.

***Key Features:***
- Minimalistic Design: Featuring a clean and minimalist interface, Minimalistic Digital ensures a clutter-free display for users, focusing on essential information.
- Customization Options: Users can personalize their experience by selecting different date formats and choosing from a variety of colors for the time display, tailoring the clockface to their preferences.
- Battery Indicator: Minimalistic Digital includes a battery indicator, allowing users to monitor their device's battery status at a glance.
- Quick Stats Access: With a simple touch of the screen, users can effortlessly toggle between various health and fitness statistics, including heart rate, steps, calories burned, floors climbed, and distance traveled.

Minimalistic Digital redefines simplicity and functionality, offering Fitbit users an intuitive and visually appealing clockface that enhances their wearable experience.

## Tech Stack
- `Javascript`: Language used to develop this application.
- `Fitbit SDK`: A toolchain used to build Fitbit OS apps and clock faces.
